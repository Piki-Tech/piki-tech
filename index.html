Get To Know PikiTech

Our primary focus is to develop software solutions for organisations who want their kaupapa (subject) understood without the need to explain why te reo (language) and tikanga (customs/traditions) are paramount to them.

He Maori ahau, he Maori koe. (I am Maori, you are Maori)

Boasting 4 Maori software developers, Piki Tech sees a need to bridge the gap between Te Ao Maori (The Maori World) and Software Development for kaupapa-driven organsations. We believe technology is the key to the future for Maori and software is the integral connection between the real-world and digital-world.
